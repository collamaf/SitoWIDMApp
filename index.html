How?

1) The Wearable Detector

An array of particle detector samples in real time the photon flux

2) The Software Algorithm

A cutting edge algorithm based on Monte Carlo simulations identifies the contribution of any organ to particle counts

3) The Results

For any organ of interest, the Time Activity Curve is computed, and thus the total absorbed dose is evaluated
